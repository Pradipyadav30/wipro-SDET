1. Asynchronous Operation

A task that does not complete immediately and takes some time before giving a result.

2. Delay Mechanism

The process includes a waiting period before the result is produced, simulating real-world time-consuming operations.

3. Promise Concept

Represents a future outcome — it can either be:

Successful (fulfilled)

Failed (rejected)

4. Success Handling

Once the task completes successfully, the result is received and processed.

5. Error Handling

If something goes wrong during the process, it is caught and handled properly instead of crashing.

6. Continuous vs One-Time Execution
One-time execution after a delay
Repeated execution at regular intervals
